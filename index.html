Course Overview

This course provides an introduction to transportation engineering principles and their application in the context of Saudi Arabia. Students will learn about the various transportation modes, the agencies responsible for transportation infrastructure, and the technical fundamentals of highway design and traffic analysis.

The material is organized using Bloom's taxonomy levels, helping students progress from basic knowledge recall through application and analysis to synthesis and evaluation. Each chapter includes learning objectives, key vocabulary, worked examples, and practice problems.

Course Learning Outcomes
  • Understand the role of transportation engineering in society
  • Identify and describe Saudi Arabian transportation agencies and their functions
  • Apply human factors and vehicle characteristics in design
  • Perform basic traffic engineering calculations
  • Apply geometric design principles for highways
  • Understand traffic safety principles and countermeasures
