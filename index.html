Transportation Engineering

Instructor: Dr. Fahad Alqahtani

CE341 course material covering transportation systems, agencies in Saudi Arabia, highway design, traffic analysis, and safety principles.

Start Learning Practice Quizzes

Course Overview

Transportation system in Saudi Arabia; transportation planning and evaluation; vehicle characteristics; human factors; geometric design of highways and intersections; basis of pavement design; introduction to capacity analysis of highways and intersections; introduction to airport planning and design; application of transportation related softwares.

Course Learning Outcomes
  • Understand the role of transportation engineering in society
  • Identify and describe Saudi Arabian transportation agencies and their functions
  • Apply human factors and vehicle characteristics in design
  • Perform basic traffic engineering calculations
  • Apply geometric design principles for highways
  • Understand traffic safety principles and countermeasures
