This course provides an introduction to transportation engineering principles and their application in the context of Saudi Arabia. Students will learn about:

- Transportation modes and systems
- Saudi Arabian transportation agencies and their functions  
- Human factors and vehicle characteristics in design
- Basic traffic engineering calculations
- Geometric design principles for highways
- Traffic safety principles and countermeasures
