CE341 Transportation Engineering

Formula Sheet for Major Exam 1 (Chapters 3-4)

Chapter 3: Characteristics of Road Users, Vehicles, and Roads

Distance during PRT

$$d = u \times t$$

Units: d in meters (m), u in meters per second (m/s), t in seconds (s)

Visual Acuity

$$\frac{d_1}{d_2} = \frac{6/X_1}{6/X_2}$$

Units: distances in meters (m)

Braking Distance

$$D_b = \frac{u^2}{254\left(\frac{a}{g} \pm G\right)}$$

Units: D in meters (m), u in kilometers per hour (km/h), G as decimal (e.g., 0.06 for 6%)

Stopping Sight Distance (SSD)

$$\text{SSD} = 0.278 \cdot u \cdot t + \frac{u^2}{254\left(\frac{a}{g} \pm G\right)}$$

Units: SSD in meters (m), u in kilometers per hour (km/h), t in seconds (s), G as decimal

Deceleration to Lower Speed

$$D = \frac{u_1^2 - u_2^2}{254\left(\frac{a}{g} \pm G\right)}$$

Units: D in meters (m), u₁, u₂ in kilometers per hour (km/h), G as decimal

Horizontal Curve - Minimum Radius

$$R = \frac{u^2}{127(e + f_s)}$$

Units: R in meters (m), u in kilometers per hour (km/h), e and fₛ as decimals

Chapter 4: Geometric Design — Vertical Alignment

Stationing

$$\text{Sta.}_{\text{BVC}} = \text{Sta.}_{\text{PVI}} - \frac{L}{2}$$ $$\text{Sta.}_{\text{EVC}} = \text{Sta.}_{\text{PVI}} + \frac{L}{2}$$

Units: Stations in meters (m), L in meters (m)

Percent Grade

$$G = \frac{\Delta\text{Elevation}}{\Delta\text{Horizontal Distance}} \times 100$$

Units: G in percent (%), elevation and distance in same units

Algebraic Difference in Grades

$$A = |G_1 - G_2|$$

Units: A and G in percent (%)

Parabolic Vertical Curve Equation

$$y = ax^2 + bx + c$$

Units: y and x in meters (m), G in percent (%), L in meters (m)

where:

  • a = (G₂ - G₁) / (200L)
  • b = G₁ / 100
  • c = Elev at BVC (m)

Crest: a < 0 | Sag: a > 0

Slope at Any Point

$$\frac{dy}{dx} = 2ax + b$$

Units: dy/dx in m/m (decimal), x in meters (m)

K-Value

$$K = \frac{L}{A}$$

Units: K in meters per percent (m/%), L in meters (m), A in percent (%)

BVC and EVC Elevations

$$\text{Elev}_{\text{BVC}} = \text{Elev}_{\text{PVI}} - G_1 \times \frac{L/2}{100}$$ $$\text{Elev}_{\text{EVC}} = \text{Elev}_{\text{PVI}} + G_2 \times \frac{L/2}{100}$$

Units: Elev in meters (m), G in percent (%), L in meters (m)

Elevation at Any Point (Tangent-Plus-Offset Method)

Units: Elev in meters (m), x and L in meters (m), G and A in percent (%)

Step 1 - Tangent elevation:

$$\text{Tangent Elev} = \text{Elev}_{\text{BVC}} + G_1 \times \frac{x}{100}$$

Step 2 - Offset:

$$Y = \frac{A}{200L} \times x^2$$

Step 3 - Apply offset:

  • Crest: Curve Elev = Tangent Elev − Y
  • Sag: Curve Elev = Tangent Elev + Y
