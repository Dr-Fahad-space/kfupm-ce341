Course Description

This course provides an introduction to transportation engineering principles and their application in the context of Saudi Arabia. Students will learn about various transportation modes, the agencies responsible for transportation infrastructure, and the technical fundamentals of highway design and traffic analysis.

The course covers the planning, design, operation, and maintenance of transportation systems with emphasis on highway and traffic engineering. Topics include transportation system components, human factors, vehicle characteristics, geometric design, traffic studies, and safety.

Course Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to:

  • Understand the role of transportation engineering in society and its relationship to other engineering disciplines
  • Identify and describe Saudi Arabian transportation agencies and their functions
  • Apply human factors and vehicle characteristics in transportation design
  • Perform basic traffic engineering calculations and analysis
  • Apply geometric design principles for highways and intersections
  • Understand traffic safety principles and crash analysis methods
  • Analyze transportation problems using engineering principles and professional judgment

Course Topics

Week 1
Introduction to Transportation Engineering: mobility vs accessibility, system components, modes of transport
Week 2
Transportation Agencies in Saudi Arabia: MOT, TGA, RGA, GACA, MAWANI, SAR, NTSC
Week 3
Driver Characteristics: visual acuity, perception-reaction time, PIEV theory
Week 4
Vehicle Characteristics: static and kinematic characteristics, braking distance
Week 5
Road Characteristics: sight distance, SSD, PSD, curve design
Week 6
Traffic Engineering Studies: speed, volume, density studies
Week 7
Midterm Exam Review and Examination
Week 8
Highway Design Standards: design controls, horizontal alignment
Week 9
Vertical Alignment: grades, vertical curves
Week 10
Cross-section Elements: lanes, shoulders, medians
Week 11
Intersection Design: types, capacity, channelization
Week 12
Traffic Safety: crash analysis, countermeasures
Week 13
Final Project Presentations
Week 14
Final Exam Review
