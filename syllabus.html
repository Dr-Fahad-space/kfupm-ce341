Course Catalog Description

This course covers transportation system in Saudi Arabia; transportation planning and evaluation; vehicle characteristics; human factors; geometric design of highways and intersections; the basis of pavement design; introduction to capacity analysis of highways and intersections; introduction to airport planning and design; application of transportation-related software.

Textbook

Garber, N. J., & Hoel, L. A. (2020). Traffic and Highway Engineering. Cengage Learning.

Course Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

  • Understand the role of transportation engineering in society and Saudi Arabia
  • Identify and describe Saudi Arabian transportation agencies and their functions
  • Apply human factors and vehicle characteristics in transportation design
  • Perform basic traffic engineering calculations and analysis
  • Apply geometric design principles for highways and intersections
  • Understand traffic safety principles and countermeasures
  • Utilize transportation-related software for analysis and design

Course Outline / Chapter Topics

Chapter Topic Description
1 Introduction to Transportation Engineering Fundamentals of transportation engineering, mobility, accessibility, system components, and modes of transportation
2 Transportation Agencies in Saudi Arabia Saudi transportation system organization: policy, regulation, infrastructure, standards, investigation, and enforcement entities
3 Characteristics of Road Users, Vehicles, and Roads Driver perception-reaction, pedestrian characteristics, vehicle dynamics, and road design elements
4 Geometric Design of Highways Horizontal and vertical curves, cross-section elements, and design controls
5 Fundamental Principles of Traffic Flow Speed, volume, density relationships, and traffic stream models
6 Capacity and Level-of-Service Analysis Highway capacity analysis and level-of-service criteria for various facility types
7 Traffic Control Concepts Traffic control devices, signal timing, and intersection control strategies
8 Transportation Safety and Road Safety Engineering Crash analysis, countermeasures, safety evaluation methods, and the Highway Safety Manual approach
9 Pavement Design Pavement types, design methods, and material considerations
10 Transportation Planning Travel demand forecasting, transportation planning process, and evaluation methods
11 Public Mass Transportation Public transit modes, operations, planning, and design considerations
12 Airport Engineering Airport planning, runway design, and airfield capacity
13 Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS) ITS technologies, applications, and future trends in transportation
